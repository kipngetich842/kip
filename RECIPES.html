North African Chicken Tagine

Tagine is a traditional Moroccan stew, typically made with meat, vegetables, and spices. This version features tender chicken simmered with onions, garlic, preserved lemons, and olives, for a savory and aromatic dish that is sure to impress.

West African Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a popular dish in many West African countries, made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and a variety of spices. This version features chicken, bell peppers, and peas, for a colorful and flavorful one-pot meal that is perfect for a family dinner or a festive occasion.

East African Chapati Bread

Chapati is a popular flatbread in many East African countries, made with flour, water, and oil. This version is flavored with cumin and coriander, and is perfect for scooping up stews and curries, or for savoring on its own.
