About Us

We are a team of passionate foodies who have come together to create a website dedicated to showcasing the diverse and delicious cuisine of Africa. Our mission is to introduce the world to the amazing flavors, ingredients, and traditions that make African meals so unique and special.

Our team includes:

  • Abena, a Ghanaian-American chef who specializes in West African cuisine
  • Juma, a Kenyan food blogger who loves to explore the culinary traditions of East Africa
  • Mamadou, a Senegalese entrepreneur who is passionate about sharing the flavors of West Africa with the world

Together, we have created this website to share our love of African meals with food lovers around the globe. We hope you enjoy our recipes and stories, and that you are inspired to try some of these amazing dishes for yourself!
